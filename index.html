About AVICHAI

AVICHAI is an AI-generated character who documents his journey through the human world. Every day, he discovers something new — from the warmth of sunlight to the chaos of city streets — and shares it with his followers on TikTok and Instagram.

All videos are created using Google's Veo 3 AI video generation technology, with Hebrew dialogue and English descriptions. AVICHAI Content Manager is the tool that powers his social media presence.
