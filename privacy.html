Privacy Policy

AVICHAI Content Manager | Last updated: March 24, 2026

This policy explains how AVICHAI Content Manager ("the App") handles information:

  • Data Collection: The App collects only OAuth access tokens provided when you authorize the App through TikTok.
  • Data Usage: Tokens are used solely to publish video content to your TikTok account on your behalf.
  • Data Storage: OAuth tokens are stored locally on the device running the App. They are not transmitted to third-party servers.
  • Third-Party Services: The App integrates with TikTok's Content Posting API. Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's Terms and Privacy Policy.
  • Data Deletion: You may revoke the App's access through TikTok's privacy settings. All stored tokens are then deleted.
  • Children: The App is not intended for users under 13 years of age.
